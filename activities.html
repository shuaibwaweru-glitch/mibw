24/7 Crisis Support Program

For Men in Crisis

When you're in immediate distress, you need help right now. Our crisis support program provides immediate access to professional resources, hotlines, and emergency support.

What's Available:

  • Direct access to 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text)
  • Crisis Text Line (Text HOME to 741741)
  • Emergency services coordination (911)
  • Safety planning tools
  • Immediate resource access
  • Post-crisis follow-up support

How to Access:

Crisis support is available 24/7. Click the crisis widget (üÜò button) on any page, call 988, or text 741741. You will be connected with a trained crisis counselor immediately.

Self-Assessment & Screening Program

Know Where You Stand

Understanding your mental health is the first step toward getting help. Our professional screening tools give you clarity and direction.

Assessment Tools Available:

  • PHQ-9 (Depression): 9-question depression screening with real-time scoring
  • GAD-7 (Anxiety): 7-question anxiety screening with professional interpretation
  • Suicide Risk Assessment: Professional-grade screening with crisis resources

What Happens Next:

  • Complete assessment (3-5 minutes)
  • Receive immediate scoring and interpretation
  • Get personalized resource recommendations
  • Connect with professional help if needed
  • Access follow-up resources and support
