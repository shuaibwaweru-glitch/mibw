Our Programs

Comprehensive Mental Health Programs

Manwell offers a wide range of specialized programs designed to address specific mental health needs and life circumstances. Each program is developed by our clinical team and delivered with compassion and expertise.

Featured Programs

Individual Therapy

  • One-on-one counseling with licensed therapists
  • Personalized treatment plans
  • Flexible scheduling and teletherapy options
  • Evidence-based therapeutic approaches

Group Therapy

  • Supportive group sessions led by licensed therapists
  • Topic-specific groups (anxiety, depression, trauma)
  • Build connections with others facing similar challenges
  • Develop coping skills in a supportive community

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

  • Structured multi-hour sessions for more intensive support
  • Ideal for those needing more than weekly therapy
  • Includes individual, group, and skill-building sessions
  • Crisis management and relapse prevention

Psychiatric Services

  • Medication evaluation and management
  • Psychiatric assessment and diagnosis
  • Medication adjustment and monitoring
  • Integration with therapy for holistic care

Wellness & Recovery Programs

  • Substance abuse recovery support
  • Wellness coaching and life skills training
  • Mindfulness and meditation programs
  • Exercise and nutrition for mental health

Family Programs

  • Family therapy and couples counseling
  • Psychoeducation for families and caregivers
  • Support groups for family members
  • Communication and conflict resolution skills

Get Started Today

Our team is ready to help you find the right program for your needs. Contact us for a consultation to discuss which program would be the best fit for your mental health journey.
