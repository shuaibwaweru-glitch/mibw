PHQ-9: Depression Screening

Patient Health Questionnaire-9

What is this? The PHQ-9 is a 9-item self-report questionnaire that assesses depressive symptoms over the past 2 weeks. Widely used in primary care and mental health settings.

Scoring: 0-4 (minimal), 5-9 (mild), 10-14 (moderate), 15-19 (moderately severe), 20-27 (severe)

Over the last 2 weeks, how often have you been bothered by any of the following problems?

0 = Not at all 1 = Several days 2 = More than half the days 3 = Nearly every day
0 / 27

GAD-7: Anxiety Screening

Generalized Anxiety Disorder-7

What is this? The GAD-7 is a 7-item self-report questionnaire that assesses generalized anxiety symptoms. It's validated for detecting anxiety disorders and monitoring treatment response.

Scoring: 0-4 (minimal), 5-9 (mild), 10-14 (moderate), 15+ (severe)

Over the last 2 weeks, how often have you been bothered by the following?

0 = Not at all 1 = Several days 2 = More than half the days 3 = Nearly every day
