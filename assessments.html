Mental Health Assessments

Understanding Your Mental Health

Our comprehensive mental health assessments are designed to provide you with a clear understanding of your emotional wellbeing, identify potential challenges, and create a foundation for personalized treatment planning.

Depression Screening

A comprehensive evaluation of depressive symptoms, severity, and impact on daily functioning. Our assessment identifies patterns and potential underlying causes.

Anxiety Assessment

Thorough evaluation of anxiety symptoms including generalized anxiety, social anxiety, and panic disorder. Helps determine the most effective treatment approach.

Trauma Screening

Specialized assessment for trauma-related conditions including PTSD. Uses evidence-based tools to evaluate trauma exposure and related symptoms.

Personality Assessment

In-depth evaluation of personality traits and patterns that may influence mental health and relationships. Supports self-awareness and personal growth.

Substance Use Evaluation

Comprehensive assessment of substance use patterns, dependence risk, and co-occurring mental health conditions.

Cognitive Functioning

Assessment of memory, attention, executive function, and other cognitive abilities to identify any concerns or cognitive strengths.

Our Assessment Process

1

Initial Consultation

45-minute session to understand your concerns, history, and assessment goals.

2

Results & Recommendations

Personalized report with findings and treatment recommendations.
