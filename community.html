Community Guidelines

Our Commitment to Safety

Manwell is dedicated to creating a safe, respectful, and supportive community. We ask all members to follow these guidelines:

  • Be respectful: Treat all members with dignity and kindness
  • Listen actively: Hear others without judgment
  • Share authentically: Your honest experiences help others
  • Maintain confidentiality: Don't share others' personal information
  • No harassment: Bullying, discrimination, and hate speech are not tolerated
  • Seek professional help: Community support supplements but doesn't replace professional treatment
  • Follow moderation: Report inappropriate content to moderators
