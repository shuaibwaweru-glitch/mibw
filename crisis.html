Crisis Support Available 24/7

NATIONAL CRISIS HOTLINE

988

Call or text anytime, day or night. Free. Confidential. Always there.

You Are Not Alone

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, suicidal thoughts, or overwhelming emotional distress, help is available immediately. Our crisis support services are here to provide immediate assistance, guidance, and compassionate care when you need it most.

Crisis situations require immediate, professional support. Manwell's crisis team is trained in emergency intervention and trauma response.

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

988
Call or text 988. Available 24/7. Free and confidential support for people in suicidal crisis and their loved ones.

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741
Free, confidential support available 24/7 via text message from trained crisis counselors.

SAMHSA National Helpline

1-800-662-4357
Free, confidential referral and information service for people with mental illness and substance use disorders.

National Domestic Violence Hotline

1-800-799-7233
Confidential support for people experiencing domestic violence, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Veterans Crisis Line

988 then press 1
Free, confidential crisis support for veterans and their families, 24/7.

International Association for Suicide Prevention

Visit iasp.info/resources for crisis centers worldwide and international suicide prevention resources.

Warning Signs to Look For

It's important to recognize warning signs in yourself and others. Some common indicators of crisis include persistent thoughts of death or suicide, extreme mood changes, dramatic personality changes, withdrawal from friends and activities, or extreme behavior changes.
