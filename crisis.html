You're not alone. Get immediate help and support during your time of crisis.

988

⚠️ IMMEDIATE DANGER?

Warning Signs

If you or someone you know shows these signs, reach out for help immediately.

🧠 Emotional Signs

  • Hopelessness or despair
  • Severe anxiety or panic
  • Intense anger or rage
  • Feeling numb or disconnected
  • Overwhelming guilt or shame
  • Talking about being a burden

🔄 Behavioral Signs

  • Withdrawing from friends/family
  • Giving away possessions
  • Risky or reckless behavior
  • Increased substance use
  • Saying goodbye to people
  • Searching for ways to harm self

💪 Physical Signs

  • Sleeping too much or too little
  • Changes in appetite
  • Neglecting personal hygiene
  • Increased fatigue
  • Unexplained physical aches
  • Loss of interest in activities

What to Do in a Crisis

If You're Having Suicidal Thoughts

  • Call 988 or text HOME to 741741
  • Tell someone you trust how you're feeling
  • Remove means of self-harm if possible
  • Go to the nearest emergency room
  • Stay with someone you trust
  • Make a safety plan with a professional
  • Remember: These feelings can change with help

If Someone Else Is in Crisis

  • Take them seriously. Listen without judgment
  • Call 911 if there's immediate danger
  • Stay with them. Don't leave them alone
  • Remove any means of self-harm
  • Encourage them to call 988
  • Get professional help immediately
  • Take care of yourself too. You can help better when supported

You Deserve Help & Support

Recovery is possible. Reach out today.
