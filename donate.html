Frequently Asked Questions

How is my donation used?

Every shilling goes directly to supporting our services: 60% for clinical therapy and counseling, 25% for community programs and outreach, 10% for research and advocacy, 5% for operational costs. See our full transparency report.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! MIBW is a registered NGO. Your donation is fully tax-deductible. We'll provide a receipt for all donations.

Can I set up a monthly recurring donation?

Absolutely! Monthly donors are our most reliable partners. Monthly supporters receive exclusive updates on the lives they're helping transform.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfers, and Paybill payments. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Can I donate on behalf of someone else?

Yes! You can make a memorial donation or give as a gift. Just mention this during the donation process.

Where can I see the impact of my donation?

We send monthly impact reports to all donors showing exactly how our funds are being used and the lives being transformed. You're invited to visit our transparency page anytime.
