Why Your Support Matters

We're a non-profit organization committed to breaking the silence around men's mental health. With your help, we can reach more men in crisis, provide free counseling, and build supportive communities where men can heal and grow.

Men's mental health remains one of the most neglected areas in healthcare. Cultural stigmas prevent men from seeking help, leading to devastating consequences. Your donation directly addresses this crisis.

What Your Donation Supports:

  • 24/7 Crisis Support Hotline & Suicide Prevention
  • Free Mental Health Assessments & Screenings
  • Peer Support Group Programs & Brotherhood Circles
  • Clinical Counseling Sessions with Licensed Therapists
  • Community Education & Mental Health Workshops
  • Research & Mental Health Advocacy Initiatives
  • Therapist Training & Capacity Building Programs
  • Emergency Mental Health Interventions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about donating to Manwell

How is my donation used?

Every shilling goes directly to supporting our services: 65% for clinical therapy and counseling, 20% for community programs and outreach, 10% for research and advocacy, and 5% for essential operational costs. We publish detailed transparency reports annually.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! Manwell is a registered non-profit organization. All donations are fully tax-deductible. We provide official receipts for all donations which you can use for tax purposes.

Can I set up monthly donations?

Absolutely! Monthly donors are our most valued partners. You can set up recurring donations through M-Pesa Auto-Debit, credit card, or bank standing order. Monthly supporters receive exclusive impact updates.

Is my payment secure?

Completely secure. All online transactions are encrypted with SSL technology. We never store your full payment details. Our payment processors are PCI-DSS compliant and meet the highest security standards.

Can I donate in someone's honor?

Yes! You can make memorial donations or honor someone special. We'll send a personalized acknowledgment to the person or family you're honoring (without mentioning the amount).

How can I see my donation's impact?

All donors receive quarterly impact reports showing exactly how funds are being used and the lives being transformed. Major donors are invited to visit our programs and meet the people they're helping.

Do you accept corporate donations?

Yes! We welcome corporate partnerships and donations. Companies can support specific programs, sponsor events, or provide matching gift programs for employees. Contact our partnerships team for details.

Can I change or cancel my donation?

You can update or cancel your recurring donation at any time by contacting our donor support team. One-time donations cannot be refunded unless there was an error in processing.
