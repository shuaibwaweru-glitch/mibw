Mueni's Journey: From Darkness to Hope

The Struggle: For three years, Mueni suffered in silence. Depression had consumed his life, and he felt completely alone. He lost his job, his relationships fell apart, and he contemplated ending his life.

The Call: One night, desperate and broken, Mueni called our crisis line. Your donations made that hotline possible. Our trained counselor listened without judgment, helped him see hope when he couldn't.

The Transformation: Today, three months later, Mueni is rebuilding his life. He's in a peer support group funded by donors, receiving weekly therapy, and recently started a new job. He's reconnecting with family and helping other men find hope too.

"Your donation gave me a reason to live. Now I'm helping other men find hope." - Mueni
