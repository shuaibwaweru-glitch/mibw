Frequently Asked Questions

Are these resources really free? +

Yes! All resources in our library are completely free. We believe mental health support should be accessible to everyone. You don't need to create an account or provide payment information to access any of our materials.

Who creates the resources? +

Our resources are created by licensed mental health professionals, including psychologists, therapists, and counselors on our team. All materials are evidence-based and regularly reviewed to ensure accuracy and effectiveness.

Can I share these resources with others? +

Absolutely! We encourage you to share our resources with friends, family, or anyone who might benefit from them. You can print worksheets, forward links, or share downloads. The more people who access mental health support, the better.

How often are new resources added? +

We add new resources monthly. Subscribe to our newsletter or follow us on social media @manwellwithin to stay updated on new materials, workshops, and support options.

Are these resources a substitute for therapy? +

While our resources can support mental wellness, they are not a substitute for professional therapy. If you're experiencing significant distress, please contact a mental health professional. We can help you find a therapist through our Find a Therapist service.

Can't Find What You Need?

If you're looking for specific resources or have questions about mental health support, our team is here to help. Contact us for personalized assistance.
