Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right therapist?

Consider their specialties, therapeutic approach, session format (online/in-person), availability, and your personal comfort level. We recommend scheduling a free 15-minute consultation to ensure it's a good fit.

What if I can't afford therapy?

Many of our therapists offer sliding scale fees based on income. Look for therapists marked "Sliding Scale Available" or contact us for information about financial assistance programs.

Can I switch therapists if it's not a good fit?

Absolutely. Finding the right therapist is important. If your current therapist isn't the right fit, you can easily book consultations with other therapists in our marketplace.
